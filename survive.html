How To Survive In Minecraft

First, collect wood and make a wooden pickaxe. Then mine stone and make stone tools. After that, collect enough wool from sheep to make a bed. A bed will allow you to sleep at night and save yourself from zombies, skeletons, and other hostile mobs.

If you go underground at night, look for coal and food. Cook the food and keep it with you while mining. Dig carefully and collect useful ores such as coal, iron, gold, redstone, and diamonds.

When you have enough diamonds, make diamond tools and armor. This will help you survive more easily and defeat stronger enemies.

Always carry a pickaxe, food, and some blocks with you. Build a small shelter before night if you do not have a safe place to stay.

Once you find iron, make iron armor and iron tools. Continue exploring caves to find diamonds and other valuable resources.

When you are strong enough, go to the Nether and collect blaze rods by defeating blazes. Then find Endermen and collect ender pearls.

Use blaze powder and ender pearls to craft Eyes of Ender. You should have at least 12–15 Eyes of Ender before searching for the End Portal.

Throw an Eye of Ender and follow the direction it points. Keep throwing and collecting it until it leads you to a stronghold. Once it goes down into the ground, dig carefully to find the stronghold and the End Portal room.

Place the Eyes of Ender into the portal frame and enter the End. Destroy the End Crystals on top of the obsidian towers first, then fight the Ender Dragon.

After defeating the Ender Dragon, a portal will appear. Enter the portal to return to your world.

Congratulations! You have completed Minecraft and can now continue building, exploring, and creating anything you want.
